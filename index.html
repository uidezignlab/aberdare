Welcome to Aberdare Hills Golf Resort

1

Imagine living in a community that offers luxurious, eco-friendly homes; acres of recreational space nestled among natural forest; privacy; security; and a host of on-site amenities and services devised to maximise the quality of community life.

2

Imagine living in a community designed with your health and happiness in mind: an environment in which to raise a family; to enjoy an active and healthy lifestyle; to relax and focus; or to retire with peace of mind.

3

Now imagine this community is situated in the heart of Kenya's Great Rift Valley, one of the planet's most outstanding geological, features and a spectacular peace setting.

You are imagining Aberdare Hills Golf Resort

- a unique development that sets a new benchmark for master-planned communities in Africa.

Whether looking for your dream holiday home, the perfect retirement location or a shrewed investment opportunity, Aberdare Hills Golf Resort will free your imagination.

Just an hour's drive from Kenya's capital Nairobi, the Aberdare Hills Golf Resort is situated in the Great Rift Valley - a 6,000-kilometre trench in the Earth stretching from northern Syria to Mozambique - and an awe-inspiring backdrop.
